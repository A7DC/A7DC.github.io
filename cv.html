About

I am an incredibly passionate British designer, interested in UI/UX design and building beautiful web experiences, with a focus on mobile. I adore minimalism and in all aspects of my work, whether design or development, seek out the most simple and elegant solutions to problems.

I have worked across various countries including the UK, Australia and New Zealand, having applied knowledge and experience of various aspects of design and development in agile environments, designing and building solutions for telcos, governments and banks.
